# A/C & Heating Service in Missoula, MT

When your climate system isn’t working, it’s more than comfort — it affects visibility, safety, and daily driveability. The right fix starts with diagnostics that confirm the cause, not guesswork.

## Common Symptoms

- A/C not blowing cold  
- Heat not blowing hot  
- Weak airflow  
- Strange odors from vents  
- Fogging windows that won’t clear  
- Noisy blower motor  
- Temperature fluctuates while driving  

---

## Why It Happens

Climate issues can come from multiple sources, which is why testing matters before replacing parts.

Common causes include:

- refrigerant leaks or low charge  
- compressor or component failure  
- blend door/control issues  
- blower motor/resistor problems  
- cooling system issues affecting heat  
- electrical faults in controls or sensors

## How We Diagnose It

### Repair
Once approved, we complete the repair and verify A/C and heat performance under real operating conditions.

## FAQs

### Can you just “top off” the A/C?
Sometimes low refrigerant points to a leak. We prefer to diagnose the cause so the fix lasts, rather than repeatedly topping off.

### Why does my heat work sometimes but not others?
It may be a cooling system issue, airflow/blend door problem, or control fault. Testing confirms which.
