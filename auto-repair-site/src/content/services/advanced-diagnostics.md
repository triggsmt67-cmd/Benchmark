By starting with accurate testing, we can recommend the correct repair the first time.

## Our Diagnostic Process

Accurate diagnostics require more than plugging in a scan tool. At Benchmark Automotive Service we follow a structured testing approach designed to isolate the true source of the problem.

### 1. System Scan and Fault Code Analysis
We begin with professional scan equipment that reads manufacturer-level diagnostic data from the vehicle's onboard systems. This identifies stored fault codes, system status information, and communication issues between modules.

### 2. Data Analysis and Testing
Fault codes provide clues, but they rarely tell the full story. We analyze live data, system signals, and electrical readings to determine whether the problem is caused by a failing component, wiring issue, or control system fault.

### 3. Electrical and Circuit Testing
Many modern vehicle issues involve electrical circuits. We perform voltage testing, resistance checks, and signal verification to confirm whether sensors, connectors, or wiring are operating correctly.

### 4. Root Cause Confirmation
Before recommending repairs, we verify the failure point through testing procedures that eliminate guesswork.

### 5. Clear Repair Recommendations
Once the cause is confirmed, we explain the issue clearly and outline the recommended repair so you can make an informed decision.
