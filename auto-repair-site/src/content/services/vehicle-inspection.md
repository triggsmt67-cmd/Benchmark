## Vehicle Inspection in Missoula, MT

A vehicle inspection helps identify wear, safety concerns, and potential repairs before they turn into bigger problems.

At Benchmark Automotive Service, inspections focus on real condition — not assumptions — so you understand what’s working well and what may need attention.

---

## What a Vehicle Inspection Includes

Typical inspection areas include:

- Brake system condition
- Suspension and steering components
- Tire wear and condition
- Fluid condition and leaks
- Battery and charging basics
- Warning lights or fault codes
- General drivability concerns

---

## When an Inspection Makes Sense

Drivers often schedule inspections when:

- buying or selling a used vehicle
- preparing for a road trip
- noticing new noises or vibration
- evaluating the condition of an older vehicle
- deciding whether repairs are worth doing

## Our Inspection Process

### Inspect
We visually check key vehicle systems and common wear points.

### Plan
